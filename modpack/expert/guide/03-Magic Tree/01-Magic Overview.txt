§2§lMagic Overview

Magic works slightly differently than the tech side of things.  You will find you have to progress in all schools of magic simultaneously.

Many recipe changes on the magic side require items from other magic schools.

Magical resource gathering can be a little difficult at first.  For instance, obtaining Thaumcraft shards.
The Ex Atris Thaumcraft tab in the Thaumonomicon can be of great assistance.
